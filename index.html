Sales Objection Cheat Sheet

"It's too expensive."

Price is only an issue in the absence of value — re-anchor to the transformation, not the transaction.

"I need to talk to my partner."

Respect that — but a decision delayed is a problem unsolved; ask what your partner would need to see to say yes.

"It's not the right time."

The Stoic asks: if not now, when exactly? Clarity on timing turns "someday" into a commitment you can both honour.

"We already have a solution."

A good solution is the enemy of the best one — ask what a 10% better outcome would be worth to them this year.

"Just send me an email."

Emails inform; conversations transform — let's spend 90 seconds on the one question that actually matters to you.
